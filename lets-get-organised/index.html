Let's Get Organised

Posted on 02 January 2016

Comments

So, this one's pretty cool. I read on Lifehacker about a new way of keeping a to-do list in a notebook/journal, which is called the Bullet Journal. You may think that's kinda weird for a computer science student to write stuff down in a book but it seems like a really neat way of journaling.

You put stuff you want to do months in advance in the "future log", for instance that task you haven't quite got around to doing yet but is not critically urgent. Stuff you're planning for the month goes into the "monthly log", for instance appointments, holidays, and birthdays et cetera. Finally, stuff you have planned for the day goes into the "daily log" as it comes up. Things like talking to someone about a feature, picking something up from the shops, or any other similar stuff. The system seems pretty rigid.

The proof's in the pudding, right? We'll see how it all goes in the next few months.
